# Elena Rostova

**Summary**: A travel writer and photographer specializing in Northern and Western Europe.

**Sources**: `europe_nordic_west.md`

**Last updated**: 2026-06-14

---

Elena Rostova is the author of *The Ultimate Guide to Northern & Western Europe: Road Trips, Fjords, and Cafes* (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`).

## Travel Focus & Areas of Study
Elena's work covers:
* Road-tripping logistics, safety, and geology in **[[iceland]]** (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`).
* Arctic hiking, surfing, and scenic trains in **[[norway]]** (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`).
* Neighborhood walks in Paris and lavender harvesting routes in **[[france]]** (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`).
* Historical markets and theatre hacks in London, alongside hiking trails in **[[united-kingdom]]**'s Scottish Highlands (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`).
