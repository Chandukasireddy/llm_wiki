# Bali 🇮🇩

Bali is an island province in [[Indonesia]] and a world-renowned travel destination.

## Overview
* **Significance**: It is the second home of full-time world traveler [[David Leiter]], who met his wife Intan (a Bali local) there.
* **Activities & Highlights**: Bali offers opportunities for bucket-list hikes, climbing active volcanoes, encountering exotic wildlife, and visiting spectacular temples and monuments.
