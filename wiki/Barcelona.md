# Barcelona 🇪🇸

Barcelona is a vibrant city on the coast of Spain, offering a unique blend of history, architecture, and beach life.

## Summers in Barcelona
Summers in the city are beautiful, with an amazing vibe. The blogger from [[Traveling German]] lived in Barcelona for 11 years, choosing to spend summers in the city and save travel vacations for the winter.

## September in Barcelona
September is one of the favorite months to visit Barcelona due to the following:
* **Crowds**: The heavy tourist crowds from August disappear.
* **Weather & Sea**: The nights start to cool down, but the sea remains warm.
* **Local Culture**: September features two of the biggest local holidays, which come with unique events and traditional celebrations.
