# Spain

**Summary**: A overview of historic cities and regional arts in Spain, including Seville and Barcelona.

**Sources**: `europe_mediterranean_east.md`

**Last updated**: 2026-06-14

---

Spain is characterized by regional diversity, distinct architecture, and active late-night street cultures (source: `europe_mediterranean_east.md`).

## Barcelona
* For detailed coverage on Antoni Gaudí's architecture, the late-September La Mercè festival, and Catalan tapas crawls, see the dedicated page **[[barcelona]]** (source: `europe_mediterranean_east.md`).

## Seville
* **Real Alcázar**: A royal palace showcasing detailed Mudéjar architecture (plaster carving, tile layouts, orange gardens). It served as the filming location for the Water Gardens of Dorne in *Game of Thrones* (source: `europe_mediterranean_east.md`).
* **Plaza de España**: A brick building with canal boating constructed for the 1929 expo, featuring tiled alcoves for each Spanish province (source: `europe_mediterranean_east.md`).
* **Flamenco**: Authentic, non-commercial Flamenco performances can be found at local *Peñas Flamencas* (cultural clubs) or small venues like *La Casa del Flamenco* (source: `europe_mediterranean_east.md`).
