# David Leiter 👤

David Leiter is an American travel blogger and photographer who runs the website *The World Travel Guy*.

## About David
* **Experience**: He has been traveling the world full-time for 10 years.
* **Blog**: Started in 2019 to document international trips, share photography, and provide free travel tips & guides for off-the-beaten-path spots.
* **Content**: All articles and photos on his blog are based entirely on his personal experiences.

## Personal Life
* **Wife**: Intan, a local from [[Bali]], [[Indonesia]] (which David considers his second home). She joins him on his travels.
* **Activities**: Together they climb active volcanoes, do bucket-list hikes, see exotic wildlife, and explore castles, temples, and monuments worldwide.

## Press & Features
David's work has been featured by and he has collaborated with:
* BBC Travel
* NBC News
* Time Magazine
