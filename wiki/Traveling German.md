# Traveling German ✍️

*Traveling German* is a luxury, adventure, and foodie travel blog.

## Blog Details
* **Website**: [travelinggerman.com](https://travelinggerman.com/)
* **Travel Style**: Unique, adventure, and luxury, with a strong preference for travel on the water.

## Author Profile & Travels
* **[[Barcelona]] Connection**: The author has lived in Barcelona for 11 years (originally planning to stay only one year). They spend summers in the city and travel during the winter.
* **[[Vietnam]] Exploration**: Spent nearly three months exploring Vietnam across two trips. Did the classic Saigon to Hanoi itinerary twice, and explored the Mekong Delta and islands in depth.
* **Other Destinations**: Documented travels in [[Cambodia]], [[Thailand]] (including [[Kanchanaburi]]), and wider [[Summer Travel|Europe]].
