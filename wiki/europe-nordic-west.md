# Northern & Western Europe Guide

**Summary**: A travel guide summary covering road trips in Iceland, hiking and train routes in Norway, metropolitan and country escapes in France, and historical sites in the United Kingdom.

**Sources**: `europe_nordic_west.md`

**Last updated**: 2026-06-14

---

This source document is authored by [[elena-rostova]] (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`). It is broken down into four distinct regional sections:

### 1. [[iceland]]
Focuses on campervan logistics along the Ring Road (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`). It highlights natural wonders including the Golden Circle (Thingvellir tectonic plates, Geysir, and Gullfoss), South Coast waterfalls, and the safety measures required for sneaker waves at Reynisfjara beach and driving F-roads in the highlands (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`).

### 2. [[norway]]
Details coastal and fjord environments (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`). Key topics include the Reinebringen Sherpa steps in the Lofoten Islands, Arctic cold-water surfing at Unstad Beach, sightseeing in Geirangerfjord/Nærøyfjord, and using the Bergen Line and Flåm Railway for scenic travel (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`).

### 3. [[france]]
Combines urban guides for Paris with scenic countryside journeys (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`). It outlines quiet areas of Montmartre, budget-friendly picnic locations on the Seine, crowd evasion hacks at the Louvre, lavender driving routes on the Valensole Plateau, and historic explorations in Avignon and Châteauneuf-du-Pape (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`).

### 4. [[united-kingdom]]
Presents highlights from London's Borough Market and Museum Row alongside rugged highland experiences in Scotland, including Glencoe hikes and scenic lookouts on the Isle of Skye (source: `europe_nordic_west.md`).
