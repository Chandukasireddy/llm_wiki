# Cambodia 🇰🇭

Cambodia is a country in Southeast Asia rich in history, culture, and natural beauty.

## Key Attractions
* **Ancient Temples**: Most famous for **Angkor Wat**, along with other historical temple sites located around the Angkor region.
* **Beaches**: Tropical beach escapes are found on the islands of **Koh Rong** and **Koh Rong Samloem**.
* **Phnom Penh**: The bustling capital city.
* **Kampot**: A sleepy riverside town known for its relaxed pace.
