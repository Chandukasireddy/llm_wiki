# Vietnam 🇻🇳

Vietnam is growing rapidly in popularity as a beach destination, slowly becoming a favorite in South East Asia. 

## Beaches & Islands
* **Comparison**: Vietnam's beaches are stunning and can absolutely compete with those of neighboring [[Thailand]], even if they don't have the same level of global fame yet.
* **Islands**: Most visitors visit the mainland and perhaps take a quick side trip to **Phu Quoc** island. However, the other islands of Vietnam are hidden gems and offer excellent escapes.

## Itineraries & Exploration
According to experiences documented by the [[Traveling German]]:
* **Exploring**: The blogger spent nearly three months exploring Vietnam across two trips, doing the classic **Saigon to Hanoi** itinerary twice.
* **Key Areas**: In-depth travel guides cover the **Mekong Delta** and the various Vietnamese islands.
