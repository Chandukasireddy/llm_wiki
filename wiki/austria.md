Austria is a Central European country rich in classical history, architecture, and traditions (source: `europe_mediterranean_east.md`).

## Vienna
Vienna is the capital city, once the center of the Habsburg Empire (source: `europe_mediterranean_east.md`).
* **Schönbrunn Palace**: The summer home of the Habsburg monarchs. Features 1,441 rooms, gardens, the Gloriette viewing deck, and the world's oldest zoo (source: `europe_mediterranean_east.md`).
* **Coffee House Culture**: Viennese coffee houses are historically recognized places "where time and space are consumed." Standard orders include a **Melange** (similar to a cappuccino) and a slice of **Sachertorte** (chocolate apricot cake) or **Apfelstrudel**. Servers encourage patrons to stay as long as they want without pressure (source: `europe_mediterranean_east.md`).
